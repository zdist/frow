Frow: [frō] NOUN INFORMAL FROW (noun) ∙ FROWs (plural noun) the row of seats closest to the catwalk at a fashion show, considered to be the most prestigious and desirable place to sit.


Redefining Fashion. FROW is a leading online fashion destination, shaping trends and culture. We provide designers worldwide a global platform to showcase collections seasonally, setting the standard for modern fashion since its establishment in 2017. With a rich heritage spanning over a decade, FROW boasts a diverse network of over 100 talented designers and 200 esteemed vendors, all contributing to the vibrant tapestry of modern fashion. Our vision at FROW is to revolutionize the fashion experience by seamlessly blending the physical and digital worlds. Through our innovative e-commerce application, we are democratizing access to the fashion show experience, allowing designers, and vendors, alike to engage with our curated selection of seasonal lines from the comfort of their own devices. As we look to the future, FROW is committed to fostering growth and innovation within the fashion ecosystem. We are dedicated to pushing boundaries and redefining the way fashion is experienced and consumed. Join us on our journey as we continue to push the boundaries of digital fashion, bringing the excitement and glamour of the runway directly to your fingertips. Welcome to FROW - where fashion meets the future.
